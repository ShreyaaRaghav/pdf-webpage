### 1. Introduction

Netflix is a global leader in online streaming, serving millions of subscribers with personalized content across diverse regions. Understanding why customers leave—or “churn”—is critical for sustaining growth and improving service quality. This case study analyzes Netflix customer data to identify factors driving churn and provides actionable insights for retention strategies.
